Hobbies

My Favorite Hobbies

Outside of school and work, I love spending my time doing things that help me relax, grow, and have fun. These hobbies are a big part of who I am.

Swimming: I've been swimming competitively and recreationally for years. I also enjoy coaching swim teams during the summer, which lets me share my love for the sport with others.

Video Games: Gaming is something I do to relax and have fun with friends. It helps me stay connected and unwind after a long day.

Reading: When I was younger I used too read all the time, but as I got older I started to grow out of it, but it's something I'd like to get back into.

Hiking: I like getting outdoors and hiking new trails. It's a great way to enjoy nature and get away from anything that is stressing me out.

Lifting: Since I quit track in highschool I've found that lifting is a great way to stay in shape and let some stress out.
